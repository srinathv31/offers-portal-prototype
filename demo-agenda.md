# Offers Portal Demo Agenda (20 Minutes)

## Audience

Business stakeholders and executives - high-level, vision-focused

## Theme

**"From Idea to Impact: AI-Powered Campaign Creation with Predictive Intelligence"**

Two centerpieces:

1. **AI-Assisted Campaign Creation** - How fast and easy it is to launch
2. **Spend Stim Simulation** - Predict revenue impact before you spend a dollar

---

## Demo Narrative Arc

**Story flow**: "Let me show you how we go from a campaign idea to predicting its revenue impact—in minutes, not weeks."

---

## Demo Flow

### 1. Set the Stage (1 min)

**Start at**: Dashboard

- Quick glance at the campaign portfolio (LIVE, IN_REVIEW, DRAFT)
- "Here's our command center. But let's start from the beginning—creating a new campaign."

---

### 2. AI-Assisted Campaign Creation (5 min) — CENTERPIECE

**Navigate to**: Create Campaign

**The story**: "Watch how AI transforms campaign creation"

**Walk through:**

1. Choose "AI-Assisted" workflow
2. Enter a simple campaign goal: _"Drive holiday spending among our best customers"_
3. Select a spending group (e.g., "Premium Travelers" or "High-Value Diners")
4. **AI generates strategy recommendations**:
   - Suggested offers tailored to that customer segment
   - Rationale for why these offers match this audience
   - Channel recommendations (Email, In-App, etc.)

**Key messages:**

- "No more guesswork. AI analyzes customer behavior and recommends the right offers."
- "What used to take a team weeks now happens in minutes."
- "The marketer stays in control—AI suggests, humans decide."

---

### 3. Customer Intelligence Foundation (3 min)

**Navigate to**: Accounts → Select a Platinum customer

**The story**: "Let me show you the data powering these recommendations"

**Walk through:**

- Show customer profile: tier, annual spend, cards on file
- **Transactions tab**: Scroll through real transaction history
  - "90 days of spending behavior—merchants, categories, amounts"
  - "This is what feeds the AI. Real behavior, not surveys."
- **Personalized Offers tab**: Active enrollments with progress
  - "Each customer sees offers matched to how they actually spend"

**Key messages:**

- "We're not guessing—we're using real transaction data"
- "Every recommendation is grounded in actual customer behavior"

---

### 4. Spend Stim Simulation (7 min) — CENTERPIECE

**Navigate to**: Holiday Rewards Blitz campaign → "Run Spend Stim"

**The story**: "Before you invest in a campaign, know what it will return"

**Walk through:**

1. Click "Run Spend Stim" - watch the simulation execute
2. **Explain each step in business terms:**

   - "Analyzing which customer groups are targeted..."
   - "Processing 90 days of transaction history..."
   - "Calculating each customer's baseline spending..."
   - "Modeling expected lift based on offer response..."
   - "Generating individual customer projections..."
   - "Rolling up to campaign-level revenue impact"

3. **Show the results:**
   - **Per-account projections table**
     - "See individual customer expected lift—not just averages"
   - **Confidence levels** (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW)
     - "We tell you where we're confident and where there's uncertainty"
   - **Tier-based insights**
     - "Diamond customers show 50% higher lift potential"
     - "This helps you prioritize where to focus"

**Key messages:**

- "Predict revenue impact before spending a dollar on marketing"
- "Transaction data from step 3 directly feeds these projections"
- "Individual-level predictions, not just segment averages"
- "Confidence levels help you make better decisions"

---

### 5. Live Campaign Results (3 min)

**Stay on**: Holiday Rewards Blitz (or navigate to Enrollments tab)

**The story**: "Here's what happens when a campaign goes live"

**Walk through:**

- Campaign metrics: 45,200 activations, $2.85M revenue, 18.5% lift
- Enrollment breakdown: Enrolled → In Progress → Completed
- "Predictions from Spend Stim are validated against real results"

**Key messages:**

- "Close the loop—compare predictions to actuals"
- "Continuous learning improves future simulations"

---

### 6. Wrap-Up & Vision (1 min)

**Return to**: Dashboard

**Closing narrative:**

- "From idea to predicted impact in minutes"
- "AI assists, transaction data informs, simulation validates"
- "Marketers move faster with more confidence"
