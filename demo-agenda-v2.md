# Offers Portal Executive Demo Agenda (20 Minutes)

## Audience

Business stakeholders and executives
High-level, outcome- and vision-focused (non-technical)

---

## Before You Begin (Executive Framing)

**Say this early, before navigating anywhere:**

> "This demo has two core capabilities I want you to focus on:
>
> 1. **AI-assisted campaign creation** — dramatically reducing time and effort
> 2. **Spend Stim simulation** — predicting financial impact before launch
>
> Everything else you'll see exists to power these two capabilities."

This sets the filter. Executives will mentally categorize everything into "supports AI creation" or "supports Spend Stim" rather than getting lost in features.

---

## Theme

**From Idea to Impact: AI-Powered Campaign Creation with Predictive Intelligence**

This demo highlights **two core capabilities**:

1. **AI-Assisted Campaign Creation** – dramatically reducing time to launch
2. **Spend Stim Simulation** – predicting revenue impact _before_ launch

Everything shown supports these two capabilities.

---

## Executive Value Proposition

- Launch campaigns in **minutes, not weeks**
- **Predict ROI before spending marketing dollars**
- Reduce risk while increasing speed
- Make offer decisions based on **real transaction behavior**
- Close the loop between predictions and actual results

---

## Demo Narrative Arc

> “Let me show you how we go from a campaign idea  
> to predicting its revenue impact — in minutes, not weeks.”

---

## Demo Flow

---

### 1. Set the Stage — Context & Problem (1 min)

**Start at:** Dashboard

- Quick view of campaign portfolio (LIVE / IN REVIEW / DRAFT)
- Establish the problem:

> “Today, launching an offer takes weeks, multiple teams, and still leaves us guessing on ROI.  
> This platform flips that model.”

- Transition:

> “Let’s start where every campaign starts — with an idea.”

---

### 2. AI-Assisted Campaign Creation — CENTERPIECE #1 (5 min)

**Navigate to:** Create Campaign → AI-Assisted Workflow

**Story:**  
“AI transforms campaign creation from a manual, multi-week process into a guided, intelligent workflow.”

**Walkthrough:**

1. Select AI-Assisted creation
2. Enter a simple business goal  
   _e.g., “Drive holiday spending among our best customers”_
3. Select a spending group  
   _e.g., Premium Travelers / High-Value Diners_
4. AI generates:
   - Recommended offers
   - Rationale for why they fit the audience
   - Channel recommendations (Email, In-App, etc.)

**Key Messages:**

- AI eliminates guesswork and blank-page problem
- Best practices become built-in, not tribal knowledge
- AI suggests — **humans stay in control**
- What used to take weeks now happens in minutes

---

### 3. Customer Intelligence Foundation — Supporting Capability (3 min)

**Navigate to:** Accounts → Select a Platinum customer

**Story:**  
“This intelligence layer exists to make AI recommendations and Spend Stim predictions credible.”

**Show briefly:**

- Customer tier and annual spend
- Transaction history (scroll once)
- Personalized offers and enrollments

**Key Messages:**

- Real transaction behavior, not assumptions
- This data powers **both AI recommendations and Spend Stim**
- Without this foundation, predictions wouldn’t be trustworthy

---

### 4. Spend Stim Simulation — CENTERPIECE #2 (7 min)

**Navigate to:** Campaign → Run Spend Stim

**Story:**  
“Before investing in a campaign, know what it will return.”

**Narration (Business-Level):**

- Identify which customers are targeted
- Establish baseline spending behavior
- Model expected lift from the offer
- Generate individual customer projections
- Roll up to campaign-level impact

**Results to Highlight:**

- Per-customer lift projections (not just averages)
- Confidence levels (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW)
- Tier-based insights (e.g., higher lift among top customers)

**Key Messages:**

- Predict revenue impact before spending a dollar
- Individual-level intelligence, not segment guesses
- Confidence levels guide smarter decision-making
- Helps prioritize where to place bigger bets — and where not to

---

### 5. Live Campaign Results — Validation Loop (3 min)

**Stay on:** Campaign metrics / enrollments

**Story:**  
“This is how predictions translate into real outcomes.”

**Show:**

- Activations, revenue, lift
- Enrollment lifecycle (Enrolled → In Progress → Completed)

**Key Messages:**

- Predictions are validated against actual performance
- Results continuously improve future simulations
- This is a learning system, not a one-time tool

---

### 6. Wrap-Up & Vision (1 min)

**Return to:** Dashboard

**Closing Narrative:**

- AI helps decide _what_ to run
- Spend Stim tells us _whether_ to run it
- Together, they enable outcome-driven decisioning

> “From idea to predicted impact in minutes.  
> Faster launches, lower risk, and greater confidence.”

**Executive takeaway:**

> "The question isn't whether we can do this —
> it's how quickly we want to scale it."

**Next step hook:**

> "Happy to walk through what a pilot would look like with your data."
